Basic Care for Cats

Learn how to provide the best care for your feline friend with these essential tips.



Grooming

Brush your cat regularly to maintain healthy fur.

Feeding

Provide balanced cat food and fresh water daily.

Health

Regular veterinary visits help keep your cat healthy.

Exercise

Play with your cat and provide toys for stimulation.
